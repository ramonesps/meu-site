Welcome to my blog! I'm excited to finally have a place to share my thoughts on software development, projects I'm working on, and things I'm learning along the way.

## Why I Started This Blog

I've always benefited from reading other developers' blogs — whether it's a detailed walkthrough of a tricky bug fix, an overview of a new technology, or just someone thinking through a problem out loud.

I want to give back to that community by sharing my own experiences. Writing also forces me to think more clearly about the things I work on, so this is as much for me as it is for anyone who stumbles across this.

## What to Expect

I plan to write about:

- **Web development** — Next.js, TypeScript, databases, APIs
- **Projects** — things I'm building and the decisions behind them
- **Tools and workflow** — things that have made me a more productive developer
- **Learning in public** — new topics I'm exploring and what I discover

Posts will vary in length. Some will be short notes, others will be deep dives.

## Let's Get Started

That's enough preamble. Thanks for reading, and I hope you find something useful here.
